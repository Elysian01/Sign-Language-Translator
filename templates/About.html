Know More About Us ?





Our Goal
Our prime goal is to create a platform for hearing/speech impaired people, which they can use to communicate smoothly at any circumstance.





Train Your Model
The user records his gestures into the model with the help of a camera, and then label that particular gesture.





Practice Your Skills
When the user communicates with an oppositite party using sign language, the pre-trained model then uses the recorded gestures to relay the desired message in the form of text or speech to the receiver. The Model currently supports 21 alphabets/words signs in ASL , It can be used by beginner user to practice his skills against pre-trained model.
